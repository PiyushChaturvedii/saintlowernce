About Us

Welcome to Saint Lowrance School

Established in 2021, Saint Lowrance School is a premier English-medium institution in Heerawala, Jamwaramgarh, Jaipur. Affiliated with RBSE (Rajasthan Board), we offer holistic education from Nursery to Class 8 in a safe, modern, and nurturing environment.

We believe every child carries a unique spark. Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and student-centric approach ensure each learner reaches their fullest potential — academically, socially, and personally.

Our Mission
Our Vision
Our Values
To provide quality English-medium education that develops critical thinking, character, and confidence in every student from Nursery to Class 8 — preparing them for a bright and successful future.
To be the most trusted school in Jamwaramgarh — a place where parents send their children with pride, knowing they will receive the best academic foundation and life skills.
We stand for integrity, excellence, respect, and inclusion. Every parent, student, and teacher at Saint Lowrance is a valued member of our family. We believe that working together creates the best outcomes for every child.
